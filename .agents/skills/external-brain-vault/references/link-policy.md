# Link Policy

## Good Links

Create or keep links for:

- recurring concepts
- active projects
- reusable procedures
- people, companies, and tools that matter to work
- repeated blockers or emotional patterns
- source-derived concepts used in multiple notes

## Weak Links

Avoid or remove links for:

- generic words
- one-time passing mentions
- terms unlikely to be searched later
- links created only because the word appears in the text

## Empty Links

Empty links are not automatically bad. They mean one of three things:

1. A useful future page should be created.
2. The link is a temporary placeholder.
3. The link is noise.

Decision rule:

- 3+ incoming links: create a stub unless clearly noisy.
- 2 incoming links: keep if the concept is meaningful.
- 1 incoming link: usually leave or remove based on context.

## Stub Shape

```markdown
---
title: "..."
date_created: YYYY-MM-DD
date_modified: YYYY-MM-DD

## 概要

## 関連リンク
```
